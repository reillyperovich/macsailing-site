Our Staff

Our instructors are all Sail Canada or equivalent certified. They are very knowledgeable and bring many years of sailing and teaching experience to share with you.

Dave Thomas

Dave Thomas is the Managing Director, a past Director of the Canadian Yachting Association, member of the Learn-to-Sail committee and former head coach of the Canadian National Sailing Team.

Nick Slaney

Nick Slaney is the co-owner of MacSailing. He has raced since he was a child and currently coaches the Optimist raceteam.

Zach Mesman

Zach Mesman has been working at Mac since he was a teenager. He is currently the head race coach and is on an olympic campaign for 49er's.

Reilly Perovich

Reilly started out taking camps at Mac at the age of 8, he soon after got a job and is now the single handed race coach.
